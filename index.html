Our Mission

Legally Apprentice is a student-led initiative dedicated to making legal careers more inclusive and accessible. We provide guidance, resources, and real-life stories to help students navigate the apprenticeship route into law.

What You'll Find Here

  • Information about solicitor and paralegal apprenticeships
  • Personal journeys from current and former apprentices
  • Tips and advice for applications and interviews
  • Support for students from all backgrounds
