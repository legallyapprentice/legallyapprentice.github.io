our mission

legally apprentice is a volunteer-led initiative dedicated to making legal careers more inclusive and accessible. we provide guidance, resources, and real-life stories to help students navigate the apprenticeship route into law.

what you'll find here

  • information about solicitor and paralegal apprenticeships
  • personal journeys from current and former apprentices
  • tips and advice for applications and interviews
  • support for students from all backgrounds
